Auto Voyage

Complete Vehicle Service Management System

Auto Voyage is a comprehensive vehicle service management system for Odoo 18.0 that helps businesses manage their vehicle service operations efficiently.

This module provides a complete solution for vehicle service centers, auto repair shops, and dealerships to manage their service operations, including vehicle tracking, service requests, provider management, contracts, and customer ratings.

Key Features

Vehicle Management

Track vehicles, their details, service history, and maintenance schedules.

Service Management

Define and manage different types of services with pricing and duration.

Service Provider Management

Manage service providers, their expertise, schedules, and performance metrics.

Service Request Management

Handle service requests from initial booking to completion with a structured workflow.

Contract Management

Create and manage service contracts with different terms and packages.

Discussion Platform

Facilitate communication between customers and service providers.

Rating & Review System

Enable customers to rate services on multiple criteria and provide feedback.

Dashboard & Analytics

Get insights into service operations, provider performance, and customer satisfaction.

User-Friendly Frontend Interface

Auto Voyage provides a comprehensive frontend interface for both customers and service providers:

  • Public Website: Landing page, services overview, and information
  • Customer Portal: Vehicle management, service booking, and service tracking
  • Provider Portal: Service management, status updates, and customer communication
Compatible with Odoo 18.0 - Leveraging latest features and improvements
